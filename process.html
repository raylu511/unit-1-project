If you’ve never adopted a pet before, you may be wondering, how does the process work? What do I have to do? How long does it take? How much does it cost? Do I have to be a certain age ?

Adopting from a shelter

Every shelter’s adoption process is different and similar in some ways. One way is if you are adopting online you will find the pet you want to adopt on the shelter's website. then the website will direct you to visit the shelter facility during their adoption hours or email them for an appointment. Afterwards You go to the shelter and ask the the person at the front desk if they have a staff or volunteer to take out the pet so you can meet with him or play with him and if you like the pet. You then go to the shelter’s front desk and let them know you want the pet, then go through an interview with one of the shelter’s counselors, If you pass the interview and the pet you chose is the right fit for your family they will ask for your photo ID, you will pay the adoption fee (the average is $25 to $200), and get copies of the pet’s vaccination records and sterilization certificate so you can get him licensed. then you take your new pet home
    Interview Questions(according to blog.petcube.com these are questions they may ask in the interview
  • What is your family situation? (how many people live in your household and do they want to adopt a dog)
  • What is your housing situation?(do you have a apartment or a house and are you planning to move in the near future)
  • What is your previous experience with pets and do you own any animals right now?(how many pets you had in the past and what happen to them, do you know how to introduce a pet into a new family)
  • What is your work situation? (are you able to supprt the dog financially, how long will your dog be alone while you are at work.)
  • What kind of lifestyle do you have? (are you an active, outdoorsy person or not, will you take the dog with you on vacations/social events/etc., are you in a relationship and how would a dog fit into that)
  • What’s your knowledge about rescue dogs? (are you prepared to train and socialize a dog, do you know that sometimes dogs end up in shelter because of abuse and neglect and never fully recover, why do you want to adopt this dog)
