Our Mission

We at E-Adopt have been dedicated to informing pet owners about options of adopting shelter animals for over a decade. After going through the long and difficult adoption process with their pets, E-Adopt founders Aldo, Damian, & Ray decided to build an online community to match animal lovers with shelter animals and streamline the adoption process. We care deeply about animals at E-Adopt, and paperwork and long applications shouldn’t get in the way of our furry friends being matched with loving and safe homes. Our mission at E-Adopt is to save as many animal lives as possible--and to spread as many resources and information about the adoption process and shelter animals as possible. We do this by connecting pet owners with local animal shelters and resources. We do this by following PETA guidelines to make sure we deliver our promise to our furry friends and you in an ethical, and efficient way.



⇝Because you'll save a life⇜

Each year, it's estimated that more than one million adoptable dogs and cats are euthanized in the U.S., because too many pets come into shelters and too few people consider adoption when looking for a pet. When you adopt, you save lives.

⇝Because you'll have a great pet⇜

Most shelter pets wound up there because of a human problem like a move or a divorce, not because the animals did anything wrong. Many are already house-trained and used to living with families.

⇝Because it costs less⇜

By adopting a pet, the cost of the spay/neuter, first vaccinations (and sometimes even microchipping!) is included in the adoption price. This can save you some of the up front costs of adding a new member to your family.

⇝Because it helps your community⇜

Adopting a pet and giving them a well-deserved home and also helps your local shelters and prevents pet homelessness. The more animals that are adopted, the more shelters are able to free up space.

⇝Because it combats puppy mills⇜
